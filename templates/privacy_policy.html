Privacy Policy and Terms of Service

1. Introduction

Welcome to MediGuard. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our biometric authentication service.

2. Biometric Data Collection

MediGuard collects and processes the following types of biometric data:

  • Fingerprints: Digital representation of your fingerprint patterns.
  • Eye Blink Patterns: Sequences representing your unique eye blink patterns.
  • Neck Movement Patterns: Sequences representing your neck movement gestures.
  • Voice Recognition: Voice samples and derived acoustic features.
  • Facial Recognition: Facial geometry data and features.

3. How We Use Your Biometric Data

Your biometric data is used exclusively for:

  • Authentication of your identity
  • Securing access to your protected files
  • Improving our biometric recognition algorithms

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your biometric data:

  • All biometric data is stored locally on your device
  • Data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption protocols
  • Biometric templates (not raw data) are used whenever possible
  • Access to biometric data is restricted to authentication processes only

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You control your biometric data. You may reset or delete your biometric data at any time through the application interface. Upon account deletion, all associated biometric data is permanently destroyed.

6. Your Consent

By using MediGuard and registering your biometric data, you explicitly consent to the collection, use, and storage of your biometric information as described in this policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@mediguard.com.
